Our Story

We started because the gap
was too obvious to ignore.

Maekido was born from a simple observation: Indian schools produce some of the world's most hardworking students — but very few are taught to design, create, question, or make. We started with Summer programs to test our ideas with real kids. Summer 2026 proved we were onto something real.

"Every child is born a maker. Our job is to give them the tools, the language, and the confidence to prove it."

Vision

To be the place where India's next generation of designers, innovators, and leaders begin.

Mission

To make world-class creative education accessible, joyful, and career-relevant for every Indian student.

Tagline

Future Makers
Start Here.

Our Timeline

How we got here

1
Early 2026
Maekido is founded
A small team of educators, designers, and professionals come together with one shared belief: Indian students deserve better than textbooks alone.
2
April 2026
First curriculum developed
Four programs designed, tested, and refined — UI/UX Design, Gamers Lab, Makers Canvas, and Project Abhay.
3
Summer 2026
Summer programs launch
80+ students. Four weeks. Four programs. 100% parent satisfaction. Maekido proved the concept works.
4
Now — 2026–26
Bringing it to schools
All 4 proven programs now available for in-school and after-school delivery. Plus 2 new programs — Design Thinking and Robotics — launching this year.
