Beginners UI/UX Design

From zero to Figma —
in two weeks.

Students with no design background learned to research users, build wireframes, and present polished Figma prototypes. Most had never heard of UI/UX before they walked in.

✓Built complete app wireframes in Figma
✓Conducted real user research interviews
✓Presented final designs to a live audience

"I designed my first app and my parents couldn't believe it was made by me. I want to be a UI designer now."

— Aryan, Grade 9 · UI/UX Design Camp

Gamers Lab · Board Game Design

They didn't just play games —
they made them.

Students designed original board games from scratch — writing rules, creating mechanics, playtesting with peers, and presenting finished games to families.

✓Designed original board game concept to prototype
✓Learned systems thinking and game mechanics
✓Ran playtesting sessions and iterated designs

"We had to think about every single rule. When people actually played our game and had fun — best feeling ever."

— Riya, Grade 7 · Gamers Lab

Makers Canvas · Mandala & Mosaic Art

Patience, patterns,
and pure creativity.

Students mastered Mandala drawing and Mosaic art — building focus, fine motor skills, and a lifelong artistic practice in a deeply calming environment.

✓Created complete Mandala artworks
✓Built Mosaic pieces using colour theory
✓Exhibited work at end-of-program showcase

"My daughter sat for 3 hours on her Mandala without looking up once. This was exactly what she needed."

— Parent · Makers Canvas

Project Abhay · Legal Literacy

Every student deserves to know
their rights.

Students explored India's legal system — their rights as citizens and minors, how courts work, and how to protect themselves. They left informed and empowered.

✓Learned fundamental rights and child protection laws
✓Ran mock courtroom sessions
✓Created awareness posters for their communities

"I didn't know I had rights this whole time. Now I can help my friends and family understand them too."

— Kavya, Grade 10 · Project Abhay
